About Beetle

Beetles are a diverse group of insects with hard wing cases called elytra. They occupy many habitats worldwide.

Fun Facts:
  • Beetles make up almost 40% of all insect species.
  • Some beetles can produce light (fireflies).
  • They play roles in pollination, decomposition, and as pests.
